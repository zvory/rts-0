# A-19 modular artillery pass 02

Fresh regeneration of the modular Soviet 122 mm gun M1931/37 (A-19) sheet.
The pass keeps exactly four disconnected components: two complete support
trails, a central two-wheel carriage, and a separate barrel/cradle/breech with
an oversized recoil assembly.

The generation prompt preserves the pass-01 pale historical-game treatment and
changes one visual requirement: the complete weapon assembly points roughly
20–25 degrees muzzle-up so the silhouette reads immediately as indirect-fire
field artillery rather than a nearly horizontal anti-tank gun.

Runtime review treatment remains temporary:

- the left trail receives a fixed purple tint;
- both complete trail crop frames carry a black rectangular border;
- the mounting-ring center is the origin for each trail;
- the SVG Artillery rig remains authoritative for setup visibility, carriage
  and weapon facing, recoil, muzzle flash, and gameplay anchors.

Generated with the built-in image-generation tool on a removable magenta key,
then converted locally to alpha with soft matte and despill. The exact prompt is
stored in `generation-prompt.txt`.
