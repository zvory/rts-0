# White Movement Review Prompt Summary

The idle repaint used each accepted field-grey sprite as a precise edit target. Only the helmet and
cloth uniform were changed to neutral matte white with light neutral-gray shading. Skin, rifle
wood, metal, leather, and the ochre Panzerfaust remained unchanged.

Each movement frame was generated separately from the matching white idle. The regular Rifleman
also used one current-production movement cell as a pose reference. Prompts limited movement to
shoulder roll, sleeve/coat compression, upper-body lean, and subtle hand/rifle bob because the
sprite has no legs.

The Panzerfaust frames used the accepted regular movement poses as motion references and preserved
the launcher on the back with restrained sway. Every accepted prompt explicitly prohibited
mirroring, switching shoulders, or swapping hands: the trigger hand remains at the receiver and
the supporting hand remains under the fore-end.

One regular movement result violated that invariant and was excluded. The final review sheets use
standing plus three movement cells and are not connected to runtime rendering.
