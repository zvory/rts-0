# Ekat Clean-Slate Brief

Status: Reserved identity only. No implementation scope is approved.

Ekat is the reserved id and name for the first future non-Kriegsia faction: `ekat`.
Kriegsia is the existing RTS faction where the player controls many units, builds a base, gathers
resources, and manages production. Ekat should restart from the opposite core idea: a
MOBA-like faction where the player mostly controls one hero unit.

The purged RTS-style Ekat content is not approved and must not be recreated without explicit
new approval. This includes workers, conscripts, signal teams, command posts, supply caches,
workshops, a standard RTS loadout, and the Mark Target ability.

Open design questions before implementation:

- Hero identity, baseline controls, and camera/selection expectations.
- Whether the hero has levels, items, ability upgrades, cooldown-only progression, objectives, or
  another advancement model.
- Whether Ekat uses Steel/Oil/Supply at all, and if so how those resources matter without a
  traditional RTS production loop.
- Death, respawn, retreat, healing, and comeback rules.
- Whether any supporting entities exist, and whether they are temporary summons, objectives, pets,
  structures, or purely visual effects.
- How the faction wins or contributes to team victory in mixed Kriegsia/Ekat matches.
- AI and prediction policy, both blocked by default until explicitly approved.

Implementation remains blocked until a later rules/balance spec answers those questions and names
the exact first playable slice.
