# Future Roadmap Reminders

These are not committed roadmap phases. They are reminders for future work that seems worth
thinking about once the current documented plans are done.

Use this filter: does the idea make scouting, army control, map control, tech choice, or battle
readability better?

## Control Groups

Basic numbered control groups would make multiple armies, workers, and production easier to manage.
Keep this mostly client-local unless there is a strong reason not to.

## Rally Points

Production-building rally points would reduce spawn babysitting and make continuous production feel
natural. Consider both combat-unit rally points and worker rally-to-resource behavior.

## Small Research Upgrades

Small upgrades could add timing choices without requiring many new units. Think infantry, MG, tank,
engineer, economy, or recon improvements. Avoid invisible stat soup.

## Broader Tech Paths

The current tech tree is compact. Future branches could support more distinct strategic identities:
infantry, support weapons, armor, economy, recon, defenses, or logistics.

The important part is not just "more tech"; it should create different plans and scoutable
commitments.

## Map Variety

More handcrafted maps would make balance and strategy less dependent on the current single map.
Think different lane shapes, expansion safety, choke density, and eventually terrain identity.

## Fortifications

Fortifications could make holding ground more interesting: sandbags, watch posts, bunkers, MG
nests, tank traps, or other field works.

Watch the turtle risk. These should make contested map control more interesting, not replace army
movement.
