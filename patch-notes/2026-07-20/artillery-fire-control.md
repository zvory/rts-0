# Artillery Fire Control

_2026-07-20_

## Changes

- Artillery Fire’s minimum dispersion radius increased from 4 to 6 tiles by default.
- Artillery Fire Control is now available at the Research Complex after researching Heavy Guns. It costs 100 steel and 150 oil, takes 20 seconds to research, and reduces Artillery Fire’s minimum radius from 6 to 3 tiles.
- Compared with the former Ballistic Tables values, the upgrade cost decreased from 300 steel and 200 oil to 100 steel and 150 oil, and research time decreased from 40 to 20 seconds.
- Artillery Fire radius can now be selected by pressing at the target center, dragging to the desired radius, and releasing, both on the battlefield and minimap.

## Playtest watch

- Artillery effectiveness before and after Fire Control, especially the precision gained from the 3-tile minimum radius.
- Reliability of click-drag radius selection on both the battlefield and minimap.
